## How to use these numbers

- State them matter-of-factly, never as a flex
- Pair success numbers with struggle numbers for contrast — ₹70L loss → 70,000 entrepreneurs coached
- Use specific figures (₹1.2 Cr, 42.7%, 4.8x) — never round to "crores" or "a lot"
- Always tie the number to a lesson, not just a brag
- For AIM/ADVAN: lead with client outcome numbers (ROI, conversion, uplift), not vanity reach stats
- Mid-point stat drops land harder than intro stats — earn the number before revealing it
